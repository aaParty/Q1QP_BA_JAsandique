Background


Our research discusses the prediction of medical costs based on the characteristics and lifestyle of an individual. But first, let us define medical costs. From the term itself it is how much an individual spends on medical treatment depending on their needs and illness. Factors like being a smoker, and the individual’s region are set as our qualitative variables as they tell us more about the individual’s qualities. Quantitative variables on the other hand are the following: the is the medical charges and their body mass index. These are variables that dwell more on numerical values in understanding the individuals.

Furthermore, a person’s health not only affects their quality of life, but also affects them financially. Either unhealthy habits or genetics cause sicknesses. Through this research we can understand medical expenses considering the factors stated below.
